# Meridian Capital — Relationship Status

**Relationship lead:** R. Ostrov (Managing Director, Client Solutions)
**Coverage since:** 2019

## Background

Meridian Capital is a long-standing limited partner across three of our flagship credit vehicles and, since 2023, our real-assets fund. Total committed capital across active mandates stands at $620 million, net of distributions to date. The relationship is anchored at Meridian by their Deputy CIO, A. Quintero, who has been the primary decision-maker since 2020.

## Recent engagement

Our last in-person was the joint working session in Houston on November 14, 2025. Quintero indicated that Meridian's board will approve their 2026 pacing plan at their March meeting and that the private-credit allocation is likely to be increased by approximately 75 basis points against total plan assets.

He also signalled, informally, that Meridian is reducing exposure to two of our competitors on the credit side and consolidating toward managers with whom they have a multi-fund, multi-strategy relationship. We are named as one of four firms in that bucket.

## Near-term asks

Meridian has asked for our help on two specific items ahead of their March board meeting:

First, a bespoke exposure report that aggregates their look-through holdings across our three credit vehicles by sector and sponsor. Portfolio analytics has delivered a draft; we are scheduled to walk Meridian through it on January 28.

Second, a conversation about co-investment capacity in 2026. Quintero raised this as a standing interest rather than a specific ticket; we should expect them to ask for named opportunities during Q2.

## Sensitivities

Meridian's board has been critical of any appearance of preferential fee treatment across its external managers. We should continue to price co-investment opportunities consistently with our published side-letter practice and avoid any informal terms that would be awkward to disclose if raised in peer benchmarking.

## Action items

- R. Ostrov to confirm the January 28 session logistics and circulate a read-ahead by January 22.
- Portfolio analytics to finalize the exposure report by January 23 for internal review before client delivery.
- Investor relations to pre-wire the February capital call against existing commitments so that it lands before the March board meeting rather than after.
