# Acme Capital Reports Third Quarter 2025 Results

NEW YORK, October 28, 2025 — Acme Capital Partners today announced financial results for the three months ended September 30, 2025.

## Highlights

Fee-related earnings for the third quarter grew 14 percent year over year, driven by management-fee growth in the firm's credit strategies and steady performance across the seasoned flagship funds. Fee-paying assets under management ended the quarter at $78.2 billion.

The firm closed its fourth flagship credit vehicle at its hard cap during the quarter. Limited partner demand was broad-based across public pensions, sovereign wealth, and insurance.

## Commentary

"The third quarter reflected the steadiness of the franchise we have been building," said the firm's Managing Partner. "We are deliberate about the pace of deployment, and we would rather be patient and disciplined than chase near-term volume in a market that is still re-pricing."

The firm reiterated prior guidance on fundraising and deployment cadence for the remainder of the fiscal year.

## About Acme Capital Partners

Acme Capital Partners is an independent alternative-asset manager with approximately $85 billion in total assets under management across credit, real assets, and secondaries. The firm was founded in 2007 and is headquartered in New York with offices in London and Singapore.
