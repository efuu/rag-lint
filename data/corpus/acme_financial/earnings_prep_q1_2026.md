# Q1 2026 Earnings — Executive Prep

**Distribution:** Managing Partner, Chief Financial Officer, Head of Investor Relations, General Counsel.

## Objective

This document is a pre-read for the Q1 2026 earnings call. It captures the numbers we will report, the messages we want the market to take away, and the questions we expect from sell-side analysts, with drafted responses. The numbers below are management-reviewed but pre-audit; do not circulate.

## Headline numbers (pre-audit)

- Fee-related earnings: $412 million, up 18 percent year over year.
- Fee-paying AUM: $82.1 billion at quarter end.
- Performance fees realized: $87 million, primarily from two credit-fund monetizations.
- Net flows: $3.4 billion of new commitments, net of $0.9 billion of distributions.

## Messages we want the market to take

Steady fee-earnings growth consistent with the prior quarter's trajectory. Credit remains the primary growth engine. We will not pre-announce any inorganic activity but will reaffirm our long-standing posture that we evaluate both organic and inorganic paths to scale.

## Expected analyst questions

**Q: Are you seeing margin pressure as you invest in the origination bench?**

A: We are continuing to invest in proportion to the growth of the asset base. Fee-earnings margin held in line with prior guidance. We are comfortable with the current pace of hiring.

**Q: How should we think about performance-fee realizations in the second half of 2026?**

A: We do not guide on realizations. The pipeline of maturing positions in the seasoned credit funds is consistent with what we described on the Q4 2025 call; the pace at which those realize is more a function of sponsor exit behavior than of any strategic choice on our side.

**Q: Are there non-organic opportunities you are pursuing in the credit platform?**

A: As we have said historically, we evaluate both organic and inorganic paths. We are not going to speak to any specific situation on this call.

## Redlines

Do not acknowledge, directly or indirectly, any specific counterparty. Do not name Lattice. Do not confirm the existence of an active process. Route any pre-call inbound inquiries about inorganic activity to the Investor Relations team.
